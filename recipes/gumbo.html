Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Description

This is a hearty stew-like dish that is normally served on cold, over-cast days.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Bell Pepper
  • Whole, cut up chicken
  • Andouille Sausage
  • Chicken broth
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Seasoning (salt, black pepper, red pepper)

Steps

  1. Brown Chicken and sausage seperately and set aside.
  2. Make a roux
  3. Add onions, celery, bell peppers to roux and cook until onions are clear
  4. Add chicken broth and cook for 1.5 hours
  5. Add chicken and sausage and cook for 45 minutes.

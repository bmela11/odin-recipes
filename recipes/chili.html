Granny's Chili

Description

This is a thick, no beans chili. This recipe makes a large amount and can be scaled down.

Ingredients

  • 10 pounds ground meat
  • 3 (16 oz) packs of Guidry's Creole Seasoning (onions, bp, celery, etc)
  • 1 can Rotel
  • 2 (8 oz) cans of tomato sauce
  • 3 (6 oz) cans of tomato paste
  • 1 cup roux
  • 10 oz chili powder
  • Salt, black pepper, red pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brown gound meat thoroughly
  2. Add creole seasoning and cook until onions are clear
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Cook 5-6 hours
  5. Season to taste

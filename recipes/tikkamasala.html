Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

This is a creamy, spicy chicken curry dish.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 28oz/800g chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1.5 Tbs minced garlic
  • 2 Tps garam masala
  • 1 tps tumeric
  • 1 tps ground cumin
  • 1 tps Kashmiri chili, sub cayenne
  • 1 tps salt

Sauce

  • 2 Tbs veg/canola oil
  • 2 Tbs butter
  • 2 small onions/1 large onion diced
  • 1.5 Tbs garlic
  • 1 Tbs ginger
  • 1.5 tps garam masala
  • 1.5 tps cumin
  • 1 tps tumeric
  • 1 tps coriander
  • 14.5 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 tps Kashmiri chili, sub cayenne
  • 1 tps salt
  • 1.25 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tps brown sugar
  • cilantro to garnish

Steps

  1. Cut chicken to bite sized pieces and combine marinate ingredients. Marinate overnight.
  2. Add chicken when hot. Brown each side. Don't need to cook all the way.
  3. Remove chicken and set aside.
  4. Add butter and onions, cook until soft.
  5. Add garlic and ginger, saute ~1 minute.
  6. Add masala, cumin, tumeric, coriander, cook ~20 seconds.
  7. Add tomato sauce, salt, and cayenne.
  8. Simmer 10-15 minutes.
  9. Stir in cream and sugar, then add chicken with juices.
  10. Cook 8-10 minutes, thin with water as needed.
  11. Garnish with cilantro.
